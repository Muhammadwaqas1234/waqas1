Frequently Asked Questions

What type of files can I upload?

You can upload almost any document or ebook format (.pdf, .epub, .azw, .doc, .ppt). If there's a format not listed here that you want to upload, please contact us at help@scai.co.

Can I talk to multiple books at the same time?

Yes, you can chat with a single book, an entire library, or a group of books. You can also create different collections such as Philosophy, Finance, Health, etc.

How many books can I upload?

You can upload up to 20,000 pages and all your books stay in the cloud to reference anytime. You can check our pricing here.

Do you have SCAI available for businesses?

If you are a publisher, business, educational institute, teacher, professor, or course facilitator and want a custom solution similar to SCAI for your audience, contact us at help@scai.co.

How is the data stored?

We store data in secure cloud storage. We do not share data between users. All your data is private to you and you can delete it anytime.

How secure is my data?

Your data is protected with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We prioritize your privacy and ensure that your information remains safe and confidential.

What languages do you support?

We support 16 languages: Arabic, Chinese (simplified and traditional), English, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Dutch, Polish, Portuguese, Spanish, Thai, Turkish, and Russian. You can have documents in multiple languages in your library.

Is there a limit on the number of queries I can make?

Depending on your subscription plan, there may be limits on the number of queries you can make per day. Please refer to our pricing page for specific details (Pricing page link).

What are the benefits of a premium plan?

Our premium plans offer additional features such as higher query limits, larger file size uploads, priority support, and more. Check our pricing page for detailed benefits.

What's the best way to reach you?

Please email help@scai.co for any more questions.
